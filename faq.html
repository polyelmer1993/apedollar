FAQ

Ape Dollars Are On Top of My Payments?

Yes! When you send sales via Ape Jaw with your traffic you get credited for each sale and paid the agreed amount. Ape Dollars is a free bonus reward program that allows our successful affiliates to also get additional gifts as a show of appreciation from us to you for all you do.

What’s The Catch?!

There is no catch. Every Ape Jaw affiliate is eligible for these free gifts. All you do is sign up with Ape Jaw and Ape Dollars, to send traffic through Ape Jaw to generate sales. Then collect your payments from Ape Jaw and cash in your bonus Ape Dollars to bring home sweet new gear as an added fringe benefit!

How Many Gifts Can I Get?

For each sale you send via Ape Jaw you earn one Ape Dollar. Spend them however you like best. Whether you want to get smaller gifts every month, or save up for a huge shopping spree around a certain holiday is entirely up to you. Your Ape Dollars won’t expire so you can carry them over from one month to the next until you have enough for the prizes you want the most.

Won’t My Wife or Roommate Find Out What I’m Doing?

Your privacy is very important to us. When you choose your reward it will be sent to you in simple non-descript packaging. The items are pristine new out-of–the-box prizes without any branding or other overt markings. Yes, you may want to tell someone you got a new iPad Pro, but they will only ever know what you choose to share with them.
